The slow restart of swimming. Martinenghi returns to the water in Milan

It is a slow, very slow restart. The snapshot of Italian swimming is this: closed swimming pools, many emptied pools and the total discomfort of the entire aquatic sector. The management costs of a swimming pool are traditionally already high, including water pumps, electrical absorptions, filter washes, if we now add the costs to prepare the prevention and sanitation tools, required by the guidelines of the Ministry of Sport, the result looks bleak. In all of Italy you can swim in less than 10 pools, with the depressing prospect that some may not even reopen again. Without the large numbers of basic activities, i.e. swimming courses and schools, it is not possible to keep the systems running, the necessary funds are lacking...
